RESTART YOUR LOVE

Welcome to the place where lovers get closer and understand better each other!

If you are already here - most probably you do not know how to spend time interesting together.

This project is a challenge. 30 tasks for couples for 30 days.

Simple rules:
1. Press button to LogIn or Register a new account
2. Choose any number from the "Task Calendar"
3. Press "DONE" button when the task is finished

We promise this will be unforgettable adventure for your couple!

Good luck and Enjoy this time with your partner!
